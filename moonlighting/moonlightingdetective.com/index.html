Professional Investigative Services since 1990

From Kalispell Montana's Flathead Valley and throughout the Northwest, Moonlighting has been providing private, government and corporate clients with confidential, comprehensive and professional investigative services since 1990. We provide undercover and surveillance services for leading law firms, insurance companies, corporate, individual and government interests in the Northwest United States and Canada.

Serving Both Corporate and Private Clients

Our corporate clients continually take advantage of our pre-employment background screening and checks, security services and administrative services. We cater to our private client's needs by providing criminal and civil investigation services, surveillance services and accident and crime scene investigations.

Proven Methods that Get Results

Federal, State and Local government agencies take advantage of our proven methods of providing undercover and security services that get results. As a full service Investigative and Security company, Moonlighting prides itself in providing industry professionals a cut above the rest.

In 2010 we started Moonlighting Bail Bonds and have become Northwest Montana's choice for all bail and recovery services. The "Moonlighting" name is well known and trusted in Northwest Montana, and beyond.

Success Means Repeat Customers

Quality performance is everything in the investigative business. For Moonlighting, success is a job well done, a client's needs met and an effective conclusion.
